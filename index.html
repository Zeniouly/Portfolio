About Me

Hi, I'm a Game Developer that goes by the name "Zeniouly". I started in the games industy in 2021 making fangames, but after a year of making them, i've switched to a more powerful engine called Clickteam Fusion 2.5 and started making original games. Over the time, I've worked for / helped small studios like CubeGames, ChargeEntertainment and now KeyKlik Software. I'm a Programmer, Game Developer and Designer.

Me and my friend PETKU! started our own game dev. studio KeyKlik Software and released our first game Death Deliverancy on March 18th, 2023. So far, we've made 4 games and still going. After the release of The Lost Eggs in April 8th, 2023. The studio took a long 8 month break. In December 19th, 2023 we came back and released a new game called Death Deliverancy 3: Recon. As the studio co-founder, i have a lot of roles. I'm the programmer, writer, designer, game director, and tons of other jobs. Me and PETKU are confident in running our own game dev studio and in how far we've come and how everything has turned out.

Right now we are working on a Multiplayer mode for Death Deliverancy 2. It will include 3 maps, 1 gamemode (free-for-all) and super responsive multiplayer! Down below you will see all of our projects we've made so far!
